# Pokemon Crystal — Complete Game Progression Guide

> Source: pokecrystal disassembly + engine/data/REFERENCE.md + Bulbapedia
> Purpose: Full walkthrough, story beats, progression gates, legendaries, post-game

---

## Part 1: New Bark Town — The Beginning

### Starting Out
1. Game begins in player's bedroom (2F of Player's House)
2. Set time/day (affects encounters, events, NPC schedules)
3. Go downstairs — Mom gives Pokegear
4. Visit Professor Elm's Lab
5. Choose starter: **Chikorita** (Grass), **Cyndaquil** (Fire), or **Totodile** (Water), all Lv5
6. See rival (Silver) spying through lab window
7. Elm's aide gives 1 Potion
8. Elm asks you to visit Mr. Pokemon on Route 30

### Mr. Pokemon's Errand
9. Route 29 west to Cherrygrove City (no trainers; wild: Pidgey, Sentret, Rattata)
10. Guide Gent gives city tour + Map Card
11. North to Route 30 (first trainer battles available)
12. Mr. Pokemon's House — receive Mystery Egg + Pokedex from Professor Oak
13. Elm calls frantically — return immediately

### Rival Battle 1
14. Silver ambushes you outside Cherrygrove (returning south)
15. **Silver's team**: Stolen starter Lv5 (type advantage over yours)
    - You chose Chikorita → He has Cyndaquil
    - You chose Cyndaquil → He has Totodile
    - You chose Totodile → He has Chikorita
16. Prize: $300

### Return to Elm
17. Return to New Bark Town — lab was broken into (Silver stole a Pokemon)
18. Name the rival, deliver Mystery Egg to Elm
19. Receive 5 Poke Balls from Elm's aide
20. Can now catch wild Pokemon

## Part 2: Violet City — Badge 1

### Route 31 to Violet City
21. Route 30 → Route 31 → Violet City
22. Dark Cave western entrance visible on Route 31 (needs Flash to explore)

### Sprout Tower (Optional but Recommended)
23. 3-floor pagoda north of Violet City
24. Battle 7 Sages (Bellsprout/Hoothoot teams, Lv3-10)
25. Sage Li at top: Bellsprout Lv7 x2, Hoothoot Lv10
26. Reward: **HM05 Flash**

### Gym 1: Falkner (Flying)
27. Gym trainers: Bird Keeper Abe (Spearow Lv9), Bird Keeper Rod (Pidgey Lv7 x2)
28. **Falkner**: Pidgey Lv7, Pidgeotto Lv9
29. **Zephyr Badge** — Flash usable outside battle; Attack boost
30. TM31 Mud-Slap
31. Elm calls; visit Pokemon Center to receive Mystery Egg from aide (hatches into Togepi)

## Part 3: Azalea Town — Badge 2

### Route 32 + Union Cave
32. Route 32 south (8 trainers; Ruins of Alph entrance on west)
33. Pokemon Center on Route 32 — Fishing Guru gives **Old Rod**
34. Union Cave (Geodude, Zubat, Onix; 2 floors accessible now)
35. Route 33 → Azalea Town

### Slowpoke Well
36. Team Rocket is cutting Slowpoke tails
37. Kurt rushes to well; follow him
38. Defeat 3 Rocket Grunts + **Executive Proton** (Zubat Lv8, Koffing Lv12)
39. Slowpoke are freed; Kurt gives **Lure Ball**

### Gym 2: Bugsy (Bug)
40. **Bugsy**: Metapod Lv14, Kakuna Lv14, Scyther Lv16
41. **Hive Badge** — Cut usable outside battle
42. TM49 Fury Cutter

### Rival Battle 2
43. Silver appears at Azalea Town exit (toward Ilex Forest)
44. **Silver's team**: Gastly Lv12, Zubat Lv14, [starter evolution] Lv16

### Ilex Forest
45. Requires Cut to navigate (Hive Badge)
46. Farfetch'd chase puzzle — guide it back to charcoal maker's apprentice
47. Reward: **HM01 Cut**, Charcoal
48. NPC gives **TM02 Headbutt**
49. Exit west to Route 34

## Part 4: Goldenrod City — Badge 3

### Route 34
50. Route 34 → Goldenrod City
51. Day Care Center on Route 34 (breeding available)

### Goldenrod City Exploration
52. Get free **Bicycle** from Bike Shop
53. Radio Tower: Pass quiz (Yes, Yes, No, Yes, No) for **Radio Card**
54. Department Store, Game Corner, Underground Tunnel available

### Gym 3: Whitney (Normal)
55. Navigate flower maze
56. **Whitney**: Clefairy Lv18 (Encore, Metronome), **Miltank Lv20** (Rollout, Milk Drink, Attract, Stomp)
57. **Whitney cries after losing** — must talk to Lass in gym to receive badge
58. **Plain Badge** — Strength usable outside battle; Speed boost
59. TM45 Attract

### SquirtBottle
60. Visit Flower Shop (NE Goldenrod) → receive **SquirtBottle** (requires Plain Badge)

## Part 5: Ecruteak City — Badge 4

### National Park + Sudowoodo
61. Route 35 north → National Park
62. Bug-Catching Contest available (Tue/Thu/Sat): prizes include Sun Stone
63. Route 36 — **Sudowoodo blocks path** (Lv20 Rock-type)
64. Use SquirtBottle → battle Sudowoodo (can catch or KO)
65. Route 37 → Ecruteak City

### Burned Tower
66. Explore Burned Tower — encounter **Rival Battle 3** (Silver, 4 Pokemon)
67. Fall through floor to B1F
68. **Legendary beasts Raikou, Entei, and Suicune flee** — they begin roaming Johto
69. Ecruteak Gym is now accessible

### Kimono Girls + Surf
70. Dance Theater — battle 5 Kimono Girls (each has one Eeveelution)
71. After winning, old man outside gives **HM03 Surf**

### Gym 4: Morty (Ghost)
72. Invisible floor puzzle
73. **Morty**: Gastly Lv21, Haunter Lv21, Haunter Lv23, Gengar Lv25
74. **Fog Badge** — Surf usable outside battle
75. TM30 Shadow Ball

## Part 6: Olivine + Cianwood — Badges 5-6

### Route to Olivine
76. Route 38 → Route 39 → Olivine City
77. Moomoo Farm on Route 39 (heal Miltank with berries for TM13 Snore)

### Olivine Lighthouse
78. Climb 6-floor lighthouse
79. Jasmine at top with sick Ampharos — needs **SecretPotion** from Cianwood
80. **Olivine Gym is locked** until medicine is delivered

### Cianwood City
81. Surf south: Route 40 → Route 41 → Cianwood City
82. Get **HM04 Strength** from sailor in Olivine Cafe (before leaving)

### Gym 5: Chuck (Fighting)
83. **Chuck**: Primeape Lv27, Poliwrath Lv30 (Hypnosis + Mind Reader + DynamicPunch combo)
84. **Storm Badge** — Fly usable outside battle
85. TM01 DynamicPunch
86. Chuck's wife outside gym gives **HM02 Fly**

### SecretPotion + Olivine Gym
87. Get **SecretPotion** from Cianwood Pharmacy
88. Fly to Olivine, deliver medicine to Jasmine at lighthouse
89. Jasmine returns to gym

### Gym 6: Jasmine (Steel)
90. **Jasmine**: Magnemite Lv30 x2, Steelix Lv35
91. **Mineral Badge** — Defense boost
92. TM23 Iron Tail

## Part 7: Mahogany Town — Badge 7

### Route to Mahogany
93. Route 42 east from Ecruteak (Surf needed for shortcuts)
94. Mt. Mortar optional (Karate King inside → Tyrogue)
95. Mahogany Town

### Lake of Rage
96. Route 43 north (Rocket toll gate during arc)
97. Lake of Rage — **Red Gyarados** (shiny, Lv30) in center of lake
98. Must battle or catch it
99. **Meet Lance** at Lake of Rage — he's investigating Team Rocket

### Rocket Hideout
100. Return to Mahogany — Lance leads you to hidden entrance under Souvenir Shop
101. B1F-B3F dungeon with ~10 Rocket Grunts
102. Password puzzle, Electrode switches (3 Electrode Lv23)
103. Lance helps with battles
104. Shut down signal broadcasting machine
105. Lance gives **HM06 Whirlpool**

### Gym 7: Pryce (Ice)
106. Must clear Rocket HQ first
107. **Pryce**: Seel Lv27, Dewgong Lv29, Piloswine Lv31
108. **Glacier Badge** — Whirlpool usable outside battle; SpAtk/SpDef boost
109. TM16 Icy Wind

### Goldenrod Radio Tower Takeover

## Part 9: Blackthorn City — Badge 8

### Ice Path
118. Route 44 east from Mahogany
119. Ice Path — sliding ice puzzles + boulder puzzles (requires Strength)
120. Find **HM07 Waterfall** inside
121. Exit to Blackthorn City

### Gym 8: Clair (Dragon)
122. **Clair**: Dragonair Lv37 x3, Kingdra Lv40 (the dreaded Kingdra — Water/Dragon, only weak to Dragon)
123. Clair **refuses to give badge** after defeat

### Dragon's Den
124. Must enter Dragon's Den behind gym (Surf + Whirlpool)
125. Reach Dragon Shrine
126. Answer Dragon Master's questions (emphasize kindness and love for Pokemon)
127. Clair reluctantly gives **Rising Badge** — Waterfall usable outside battle
128. TM24 DragonBreath
129. **All 8 Johto badges obtained**

## Part 11: Kanto Post-Game

### Getting to Kanto
141. Professor Elm gives **S.S. Ticket**
142. Board S.S. Aqua at Olivine City port → arrives in Vermilion City
143. Alternatively: complete Copycat sidequest for Magnet Train Pass (Goldenrod ↔ Saffron)

### Kanto Gym Order (Flexible)
Players can challenge Kanto gyms in almost any order. Common recommended order:

144. **Lt. Surge** (Vermilion) — Electric: Voltorb Lv44, Electrode Lv40 x2, Magneton Lv40, Electabuzz Lv46
145. **Sabrina** (Saffron) — Psychic: Espeon Lv46, Mr. Mime Lv46, Alakazam Lv48
146. **Erika** (Celadon) — Grass: Tangela Lv42, Jumpluff Lv41, Victreebel Lv46, Bellossom Lv46
147. **Janine** (Fuchsia) — Poison: Crobat Lv36, Weezing Lv36, Ariados Lv33 x2, Venomoth Lv39
148. **Brock** (Pewter) — Rock: Graveler Lv41, Rhyhorn Lv41, Omastar Lv42, Kabutops Lv42, Onix Lv44
149. **Misty** (Cerulean) — Water: Golduck Lv42, Quagsire Lv42, Lapras Lv44, Starmie Lv47 (must complete Power Plant quest first)
150. **Blaine** (Seafoam Islands) — Fire: Magcargo Lv45, Magmar Lv45, Rapidash Lv50
151. **Blue** (Viridian) — Mixed: Pidgeot Lv56, Alakazam Lv54, Rhydon Lv56, Gyarados Lv58, Exeggutor Lv55, Arcanine Lv58

### Kanto Sidequests
- **Power Plant**: Find Machine Part (stolen by Rocket Grunt in Cerulean Gym pool), restore power, unlock Kanto radio
- **Snorlax**: Two Snorlax block paths (Route 12, Route 16); need Poke Flute radio channel (EXPN Card from Lavender Radio Tower after Power Plant quest)
- **Copycat's Doll**: Get Lost Item from Vermilion Fan Club → return to Copycat in Saffron → receive Magnet Train Pass
- **Mt. Moon Monday Night**: Clefairy dance event → free Moon Stone

## Part 12: Legendary Pokemon

### Roaming Beasts: Raikou and Entei
- **Trigger**: Explore Burned Tower B1F in Ecruteak City
- **Encounter**: Roam randomly through Johto routes; flee immediately in battle
- **Level**: 40
- **Catch Tips**: Use Mean Look to prevent fleeing; damage carries over between encounters; use Master Ball or Fast Ball
- **Raikou**: Electric; Thunder, Roar, Quick Attack, Spark
- **Entei**: Fire; Fire Spin, Stomp, Flamethrower, Roar

### Suicune (Crystal-specific)
- **Crystal**: Suicune does NOT roam; instead, fixed encounters:
  1. Burned Tower (Ecruteak) — flees
  2. Cianwood City (north) — flees
  3. Route 42 — flees
  4. Vermilion City — flees
  5. Route 14 — flees
  6. **Tin Tower** (after Clear Bell from Radio Tower) — battle at Lv40
- **Gold/Silver**: Suicune roams like Raikou and Entei

### Lugia
- **Location**: Whirl Islands (Route 41)
- **Requirements**: Surf + Whirlpool + Waterfall + Silver Wing
- **Silver Wing**: Given after Radio Tower crisis (Silver version); in Crystal, obtained from old man in Pewter City after all 16 badges
- **Level**: 60
- **Moves**: Aeroblast, Rain Dance, Hydro Pump, Swift

### Ho-Oh
- **Location**: Tin Tower (Ecruteak City), 10F
- **Requirements**: Rainbow Wing
- **Rainbow Wing**: Given after Radio Tower crisis (Gold version); in Crystal, obtained from old man in Pewter City after all 16 badges
- **Level**: 60
- **Moves**: Sacred Fire, Sunny Day, Fire Blast, Swift
- **Crystal Note**: Must also defeat all 3 Kimono Girls at Tin Tower entrance

### Celebi (Crystal Japan only / Virtual Console)
- **Location**: Ilex Forest Shrine
- **Requirements**: GS Ball (event item; in Virtual Console release, obtained automatically after entering Hall of Fame)
- **Level**: 30
- **Moves**: Leech Seed, Recover, Heal Bell, Safeguard

## Part 13: The Final Challenge — Trainer Red

### Mt. Silver
152. After obtaining all 16 badges, Mt. Silver entrance opens (Route 28)
153. Multi-floor cave dungeon with high-level wild Pokemon (Larvitar, Ursaring, Sneasel, Misdreavus, Pupitar)

### Trainer Red
154. Summit of Mt. Silver
155. Red says nothing — "..." — and battle begins
156. **Red's Team**:
    - Pikachu Lv81
    - Espeon Lv73
    - Snorlax Lv75
    - Venusaur Lv77
    - Charizard Lv77
    - Blastoise Lv77
157. Highest-level trainer in the game
158. Defeating Red triggers credits again — the true ending
