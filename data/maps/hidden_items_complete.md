## Summary Statistics

- **Total hidden items: 84**
- **Johto hidden items: 50**
- **Kanto hidden items: 34**
- **Most valuable hidden items:** Rare Candy (x5), Max Revive (x2), Berserk Gene (x1), Machine Part (x1 — quest item), Carbos (x2), Calcium (x1), PP Up (x2), Protein (x1), Iron (x1)
- **Most common hidden item:** Potions/healing items dominate (Full Restore, Max Potion, Hyper Potion, etc.)

## Notes

- Hidden items require the Itemfinder (not available in Crystal) or trial-and-error to find
- The Machine Part in Cerulean Gym is a quest-critical hidden item for the Power Plant restoration sidequest
- The Berserk Gene in Cerulean City is unique and cannot be obtained elsewhere
- Mt. Moon Square's Moon Stone regenerates (available every Monday night during the Clefairy dance event)
- National Park's Full Heal is present in both the normal and Bug Contest versions of the map
