## Cities and Towns

### New Bark Town
- **Slogan**: "The Town Where Winds of a New Beginning Blow"
- **Population**: 11
- **Connections**: West to Route 29; East to Route 27 (requires Surf, late-game)
- **Buildings**: Player's House (2F), Professor Elm's Lab, Rival's House
- **Key NPCs**: Mom (Pokegear, money saving), Professor Elm (starter, Master Ball post-8 badges, S.S. Ticket post-E4), Elm's Aide (Potion, 5 Poke Balls)
- **Items**: Pokegear, Potion, Berry (on starter), 5 Poke Balls (after rival battle), Everstone (after Togepi hatches), Master Ball (after 8 badges), S.S. Ticket (post-game)
- **Wild Pokemon (Surf)**: Tentacool Lv15-24 (90%), Tentacruel Lv20-24 (10%)
- **Story Events**: Game start, receive starter (Chikorita/Cyndaquil/Totodile Lv5), Mr. Pokemon errand, egg delivery, name rival
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Cherrygrove City
- **Slogan**: "The City of Cute, Fragrant Flowers"
- **Population**: 22
- **Connections**: East to Route 29; North to Route 30
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Poke Mart, Guide Gent's House
- **Key NPCs**: Guide Gent (Map Card), Mystic Water Man (island, requires Surf)
- **Items**: Map Card (Guide Gent), Mystic Water (Surf required)
- **Mart**: Poke Ball $200, Potion $300, Antidote $100, Parlyz Heal $200, Awakening $250
- **Wild Pokemon (Surf)**: Tentacool Lv15-24 (90%), Tentacruel Lv20-24 (10%)
- **Story Events**: Guide Gent tour, first Pokemon Center heal, Rival Battle 1 (Silver Lv5 starter, on return from Mr. Pokemon)
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Violet City
- **Slogan**: "The City of Nostalgic Scents"
- **Population**: 52
- **Connections**: East to Route 31; South to Route 32; West to Route 36
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Poke Mart, Violet Gym, Sprout Tower, Earl's Pokemon Academy, Trade House
- **Key NPCs**: Falkner (Gym Leader), Earl Dervish (Academy), Sage Li (HM05 Flash), Elm's Aide (Mystery Egg post-gym), Kyle/Rudy (trades Onix for Bellsprout)
- **Gym**: Falkner (Flying) — Pidgey Lv7, Pidgeotto Lv9 — Zephyr Badge + TM31 Mud-Slap
- **Items**: HM05 Flash (Sprout Tower), TM31 Mud-Slap (gym), Mystery Egg (hatches Togepi), PP Up (Surf), Rare Candy (Surf)
- **Mart**: Poke Ball, Potion, Escape Rope, Antidote, Parlyz Heal, Awakening, X Defend, X Attack, X Speed, Flower Mail
- **Wild Pokemon (Surf)**: Poliwag Lv15-24 (90%), Poliwhirl Lv20-24 (10%)
- **Story Events**: Sprout Tower (7 Sages, HM05), Gym 1 battle, receive Mystery Egg
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Azalea Town
- **Slogan**: "Where People and Pokemon Live in Happy Harmony!"
- **Population**: 31
- **Connections**: East to Route 33; West to Ilex Forest
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Poke Mart, Azalea Gym, Kurt's House, Charcoal Kiln, Slowpoke Well
- **Key NPCs**: Bugsy (Gym Leader), Kurt (Apricorn Ball maker, gives Lure Ball), Charcoal Maker (HM01 Cut after Farfetch'd quest)
- **Gym**: Bugsy (Bug) — Metapod Lv14, Kakuna Lv14, Scyther Lv16 — Hive Badge + TM49 Fury Cutter
- **Apricorn Balls**: Red->Level, Yellow->Moon, Blue->Lure, Green->Friend, Pink->Love, White->Fast, Black->Heavy
- **Items**: White Apricorn (daily), Lure Ball (Kurt), HM01 Cut, Charcoal, Full Heal (hidden)
- **Mart**: Poke Ball, Potion, Super Potion, Escape Rope, Repel, Antidote, Parlyz Heal, Charcoal $9800, Flower Mail
- **Story Events**: Team Rocket Slowpoke Well (3 Grunts + Exec Proton), gym battle, Rival Battle 2 (Gastly Lv12, Zubat Lv14, starter Lv16)
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Goldenrod City
- **Slogan**: "The Festive City of Opulent Charm"
- **Population**: 126 (largest city in Johto)
- **Connections**: North to Route 35; South to Route 34
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Goldenrod Gym, Department Store (6F), Radio Tower (5F), Game Corner, Magnet Train Station, Underground Tunnel, Bike Shop, Bill's House, Flower Shop, Name Rater, Friendship Checker
- **Key NPCs**: Whitney (Gym Leader), Bill (Eevee Lv20), Bike Shop Owner (free Bicycle), Flower Shop Lady (SquirtBottle), Name Rater, Radio Quiz Host
- **Gym**: Whitney (Normal) — Clefairy Lv18 (Encore, DoubleSlap, Mimic, Metronome), Miltank Lv20 (Stomp, Attract, Milk Drink, Rollout) — Plain Badge + TM45 Attract. Whitney cries after losing; talk to her again to get badge.
- **Department Store**: 2F Trainer's Market, 3F Battle items, 4F Vitamins ($9800 ea), 5F TMs (elemental punches $3000), 6F Rooftop vending, B1F tunnel access
- **Game Corner**: Abra Lv10 (200 coins), Ekans/Sandshrew Lv10 (700), Dratini Lv10 (2100), TM25 Thunder/TM14 Blizzard/TM38 Fire Blast (5500 each)
- **Items**: Bicycle (free), SquirtBottle (after Whitney), Eevee Lv20 (Bill, after Ecruteak), Radio Card, Coin Case, TM45 Attract
- **Story Events**: Gym battle (Miltank infamous), SquirtBottle for Sudowoodo, later Radio Tower Rocket takeover (5 floors, Underground, Executives Proton/Ariana/Archer)
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Ecruteak City
- **Slogan**: "A Historical City Where the Past Meets the Present"
- **Population**: 46
- **Connections**: South to Route 37; West to Route 38; East to Route 42
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Ecruteak Gym, Burned Tower, Tin Tower (Bell Tower), Dance Theater, Bill's Family Home, 2 NPC houses
- **Key NPCs**: Morty (Gym Leader), 5 Kimono Girls (Eevee evolutions), Bill (meet here first), Eusine (Suicune hunter)
- **Gym**: Morty (Ghost) — Gastly Lv21, Haunter Lv21, Haunter Lv23, Gengar Lv25 — Fog Badge + TM30 Shadow Ball. Invisible floor puzzle.
- **Items**: HM03 Surf (old man after Kimono Girls), TM30 Shadow Ball (gym)
- **Story Events**: Burned Tower exploration (legendary beasts Raikou/Entei/Suicune flee), Rival Battle 3 (4 Pokemon), Kimono Girls, meet Bill
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Olivine City
- **Slogan**: "The Port of Slithering Seas"
- **Population**: 47
- **Connections**: North to Route 39; South to Route 40 (Surf required)
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Olivine Gym, Olivine Lighthouse (6F), S.S. Aqua Port (post-game), Poke Mart, Cafe, 2 NPC houses
- **Key NPCs**: Jasmine (Gym Leader), Sailor (HM04 Strength in cafe), Fisherman (Good Rod)
- **Gym**: Jasmine (Steel) — Magnemite Lv30 x2, Steelix Lv35 — Mineral Badge + TM23 Iron Tail. Cannot battle until medicine delivered.
- **Items**: HM04 Strength (cafe sailor), Good Rod (fisherman), TM23 Iron Tail (gym)
- **Story Events**: Lighthouse climb (Jasmine + sick Ampharos), must Surf to Cianwood for medicine, deliver medicine to unlock gym
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Cianwood City
- **Slogan**: "A City That Dances with Crashing Waves"
- **Population**: 27
- **Connections**: North to Route 41 (Surf required to leave)
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Cianwood Gym, Cianwood Pharmacy, Photo Studio, 1 NPC house
- **Key NPCs**: Chuck (Gym Leader), Pharmacist (SecretPotion), Chuck's Wife (HM02 Fly)
- **Gym**: Chuck (Fighting) — Primeape Lv27, Poliwrath Lv30 — Storm Badge + TM01 DynamicPunch
- **Items**: SecretPotion (pharmacy), HM02 Fly (Chuck's wife), TM01 DynamicPunch (gym)
- **Story Events**: Gym battle, obtain SecretPotion, Fly back to Olivine
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Mahogany Town
- **Slogan**: "Welcome to the Home of the Ninja"
- **Population**: 26
- **Connections**: West to Route 42; North to Route 43; East to Route 44
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Mahogany Gym, Rocket Hideout (under Souvenir Shop), Souvenir Shop, 1 NPC house
- **Key NPCs**: Pryce (Gym Leader), Lance (assists in Rocket Hideout)
- **Gym**: Pryce (Ice) — Seel Lv27, Dewgong Lv29, Piloswine Lv31 — Glacier Badge + TM16 Icy Wind. Must clear Rocket HQ first.
- **Items**: HM06 Whirlpool (Lance in Rocket HQ), TM46 Thief (Rocket HQ), TM16 Icy Wind (gym)
- **Story Events**: Lake of Rage Red Gyarados, meet Lance, Rocket Hideout infiltration (3 floors, Electrode puzzle), gym battle
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Blackthorn City
- **Slogan**: "A Quiet Mountain Retreat"
- **Population**: 40
- **Connections**: West to Ice Path; South to Route 45
- **Buildings**: Pokemon Center, Blackthorn Gym, Dragon's Den entrance, Move Deleter, Move Tutor, 1 NPC house
- **Key NPCs**: Clair (Gym Leader), Dragon Master (Dragon's Den quiz), Move Deleter, Move Tutor
- **Gym**: Clair (Dragon) — Dragonair Lv37 x3, Kingdra Lv40 — Rising Badge + TM24 DragonBreath. Clair refuses badge; must pass Dragon's Den quiz.
- **Items**: TM24 DragonBreath (gym, after Dragon's Den)
- **Story Events**: Gym battle, Dragon's Den quiz, final badge
- **Fly Point**: Yes

## Routes

### Route 29
- **Connections**: East to New Bark Town; West to Cherrygrove City; North to Route 46 (one-way ledges down)
- **Trainers**: None (first route)
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Pidgey Lv2-4 (55% morn/day), Sentret Lv2-3 (40% morn/day), Hoothoot Lv2-4 (85% night), Rattata Lv2-4 (5%/5%/15%), Hoppip Lv3 (5% morn/day)
- **Items**: Berry (hidden)

### Route 30
- **Connections**: South to Cherrygrove; North to Route 31; Northeast to Mr. Pokemon's House
- **Trainers**: Youngster Joey (Rattata Lv4), Bug Catcher Don (Caterpie Lv3 x2)
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Pidgey Lv2-4, Caterpie/Weedle Lv3-4 (version-dependent), Ledyba Lv3 (morn), Spinarak Lv3 (night), Hoothoot Lv3-4 (night)
- **Key Location**: Mr. Pokemon's House (Mystery Egg + Pokedex from Oak)

### Route 31
- **Connections**: West to Route 30; East to Violet City; Dark Cave (western entrance)
- **Trainers**: Bug Catcher Wade (Caterpie Lv2, Weedle Lv2; Pokegear rematch)
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Pidgey, Caterpie/Weedle, Bellsprout, Poliwag (night)
- **Items**: Bitter Berry, Poke Ball (hidden)

### Route 32
- **Connections**: North to Violet City; West to Ruins of Alph; South to Union Cave
- **Trainers**: 8 trainers including Bird Keepers, Fishermen, Campers
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Ekans Lv4-6 (morn/day), Bellsprout Lv4-6, Mareep Lv6, Hoppip Lv4-6 (morn/day), Wooper Lv4 (night)

### Route 33

### Route 35

## Dungeons and Special Areas

### Sprout Tower
- **Location**: North of Violet City
- **Floors**: 3
- **Theme**: Buddhist pagoda with flexible central pillar; Sages train Bellsprout
- **Trainers**: 7 Sages using Bellsprout (Lv3-7) and Hoothoot (Lv7-10)
- **Boss**: Sage Li — Bellsprout Lv7 x2, Hoothoot Lv10
- **Items**: Paralyze Heal, X Defend, Potion, Escape Rope, HM05 Flash (from Sage Li)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Rattata, Gastly (night)
- **Puzzle**: None; straightforward dungeon

### Slowpoke Well
- **Location**: Northeast of Azalea Town
- **Floors**: 2 (B2F requires Surf + Strength, late-game)
- **Trainers**: 3 Rocket Grunts + Executive Proton
  - Grunt 1: Rattata Lv9, Zubat Lv9
  - Grunt 2: Zubat Lv9, Ekans Lv11
  - Grunt 3: Rattata Lv7, Zubat Lv9 x2
  - Executive Proton: Zubat Lv8, Koffing Lv12
- **Items**: Super Potion, TM18 Rain Dance, King's Rock (B2F, late-game)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Slowpoke
- **Story**: Team Rocket cutting Slowpoke tails; Kurt rushes to rescue them

### Ruins of Alph
- **Location**: West of Route 32, south of Route 36
- **Theme**: Archaeological site with letter-shaped Pokemon (Unown)
- **Puzzle Rooms**: 4 sliding puzzles (Kabuto, Aerodactyl, Ho-Oh, Omanyte)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Unown (26 forms, A-Z; appears after solving puzzles)
- **Items**: Each puzzle room has hidden items; Research Center has analysis tools
- **Notes**: Mostly optional; completing all puzzles unlocks all Unown forms

### Union Cave
- **Location**: Between Route 32 and Route 33
- **Floors**: 3 (1F, B1F main path; B2F requires Surf, has Friday Lapras)
- **Trainers**: 6 trainers (Hikers, Pokemaniac, Firebreather)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Geodude, Zubat, Onix, Sandshrew, Rattata; B2F: Golbat, Wooper
- **Items**: Great Ball, Potion, TM39 Swift, X Attack, X Defend
- **Special**: Lapras appears in B2F every Friday (Lv20, only one)

### Ilex Forest
- **Location**: Between Azalea Town and Route 34
- **Requires**: HM01 Cut (Hive Badge)
- **Puzzle**: Farfetch'd chase — guide the bird back to the charcoal maker's apprentice using directional approach
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Oddish, Paras, Caterpie, Metapod, Pidgey, Weedle, Kakuna, Zubat (night)
- **Items**: HM01 Cut (from apprentice after Farfetch'd), TM02 Headbutt (from NPC), Revive (hidden)
- **Key Location**: Ilex Forest Shrine (Celebi event with GS Ball in Crystal JP)

### National Park
- **Location**: Between Route 35 and Route 36
- **Bug-Catching Contest**: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday
  - Entry: 1 Pokemon + 20 Sport Balls, 20-minute time limit
  - Wild Pokemon (contest only): Caterpie, Metapod, Butterfree, Weedle, Kakuna, Beedrill, Scyther, Pinsir, Venonat, Paras
  - Prizes: 1st Sun Stone, 2nd Everstone, 3rd Gold Berry
- **Wild Pokemon (non-contest)**: Caterpie, Metapod, Sunkern (day), Ledyba (morn), Spinarak (night)
- **Items**: Parlyz Heal (hidden), Quick Claw (hidden)

### Burned Tower
- **Location**: Ecruteak City
- **Floors**: 2 (1F and B1F)
- **Trainers**: Rival Battle 3 (Silver); Firebreather Ned, Firebreather Bill
- **Wild Pokemon**: Rattata, Koffing, Zubat (night: Magmar)
- **Story Events**: Fall through floor to B1F; encounter Raikou, Entei, Suicune — all three flee and begin roaming Johto. Must explore before Ecruteak Gym is accessible.
- **Items**: HP Up, TM20 Endure (hidden)

### Olivine Lighthouse
- **Location**: Olivine City
- **Floors**: 6
- **Trainers**: 7 trainers (Sailors, Gentlemen, Bird Keepers, Lasses)
- **Wild Pokemon**: None
- **Story**: Jasmine tends to sick Ampharos at top; need SecretPotion from Cianwood to cure it
- **Items**: Rare Candy, Super Potion, Ether, TM34 Swagger

### Mt. Mortar
- **Location**: Route 42 (3 entrances)
- **Floors**: 3 (requires Surf, Waterfall, Strength for full exploration)
- **Trainers**: Multiple Blackbelts + Karate King (gives Tyrogue if defeated)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Golbat, Machop, Machoke, Geodude, Graveler, Rattata, Raticate, Marill (Surf)
- **Items**: Max Revive, Escape Rope, Hyper Potion, Carbos, Nugget, Iron, Full Restore, Dragon Scale
- **Special**: Karate King battle — reward is Tyrogue (only way to obtain)

### Rocket Hideout (Team Rocket HQ)
- **Location**: Under Mahogany Town Souvenir Shop
- **Floors**: B1F-B3F
- **Trainers**: ~10 Rocket Grunts + Executives
- **Puzzle**: Password doors, Electrode switches (3 Electrode at Lv23 — can catch or KO)
- **Story**: Lance assists player; must shut down signal forcing Magikarp to evolve
- **Items**: HM06 Whirlpool (from Lance), TM46 Thief, Hyper Potion, Full Heal, Nugget, X Special, Dire Hit, Ice Heal
- **Wild Pokemon**: Geodude, Voltorb, Koffing, Rattata

### Lake of Rage
- **Location**: North of Route 43
- **Wild Pokemon (Grass)**: Magikarp, Gyarados (Surf)
- **Special**: Red Gyarados (shiny, Lv30) — guaranteed encounter in center of lake. Can be caught or KO'd.
- **Items**: Red Scale (from Red Gyarados; trade to Mr. Pokemon for EXP Share), TM10 Hidden Power (from fisher)
- **Story**: Meet Lance here; triggers Rocket Hideout subplot
- **Fly Point**: Yes

### Ice Path
- **Location**: Between Route 44 and Blackthorn City
- **Floors**: 4 (1F, B1F, B2F, B3F)
- **Requires**: HM04 Strength
- **Puzzle**: Sliding ice floors + boulder pushing. Must solve ice puzzles to navigate through.
- **Trainers**: None (purely puzzle/exploration)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Golbat, Swinub, Jynx, Delibird
- **Items**: HM07 Waterfall, Full Heal, Max Potion, Iron, PP Up, TM44 Rest, Protein (hidden), Revive (hidden)

### Dragon's Den
- **Location**: Behind Blackthorn Gym (cave entrance)
- **Requires**: Surf + Whirlpool
- **Trainers**: Cooltrainers with Dragon-types
- **Wild Pokemon (Surf)**: Magikarp, Dratini (fishing: Magikarp, Dratini, Dragonair)
- **Story**: After beating Clair, she refuses badge; player must visit Dragon Shrine and answer Master's questions correctly. Correct answers: all answers should emphasize kindness/love/friendship.
- **Items**: Dragon Fang (from Master), TM24 DragonBreath (from Clair after quiz)
- **Special**: Only place to fish for Dratini/Dragonair in the wild

### Whirl Islands
- **Location**: Route 41 (4 entrances)
- **Requires**: Surf + Whirlpool + Waterfall (for Lugia encounter)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Golbat, Seel, Krabby, Horsea
- **Legendary**: Lugia Lv60 (requires Silver Wing from Radio Tower story in Silver/Crystal)
- **Items**: Full Restore, Calcium, Max Revive, Ultra Ball
- **Notes**: Complex multi-entrance dungeon; must find correct path to reach Lugia

### Tin Tower (Bell Tower)
- **Location**: Ecruteak City
- **Requires**: Rainbow Wing (Gold) / Clear Bell (Crystal)
- **Floors**: 10
- **Wild Pokemon**: Rattata, Gastly (night)
- **Legendary**: Ho-Oh Lv60 (Gold: Rainbow Wing from Radio Tower; Crystal: Clear Bell + defeat 3 Kimono Girls)
- **Items**: Full Heal, Escape Rope, Ultra Ball, Full Restore, Max Revive, Nugget, PP Up, Rare Candy, Max Elixir, HP Up
- **Notes**: In Crystal, Suicune is encountered at the base of Tin Tower instead of roaming; must have Clear Bell

### Dark Cave
- **Location**: Two entrances — west from Route 31, east from Route 46
- **Requires**: Flash (recommended), Surf, Waterfall, Rock Smash, Strength (for full exploration)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Golbat, Geodude, Graveler, Teddiursa (morn), Ursaring, Wobbuffet, Dunsparce (rare)
- **Items**: Potion, Full Heal, Hyper Potion, Revive, TM13 Snore, Blackglasses

### Victory Road
- **Location**: Between Route 26 and Indigo Plateau
- **Requires**: All 8 badges (guard check), Surf
- **Wild Pokemon**: Graveler Lv34, Rhyhorn Lv32, Onix Lv33, Golbat Lv34, Sandslash Lv35, Rhydon Lv35
- **Trainers**: Multiple Cooltrainers + Rival Battle 5 (Silver, 6 Pokemon)
- **Items**: TM26 Earthquake, Full Heal, HP Up, Max Revive, TM22 SolarBeam (hidden)

### Tohjo Falls
- **Location**: Route 27 (between Johto and Kanto)
- **Requires**: Waterfall
- **Wild Pokemon**: Zubat, Golbat, Raticate, Slowpoke
- **Items**: Moon Stone
- **Notes**: Gateway between Johto and Kanto; contains Waterfall obstacle

### Silver Cave (Mt. Silver)
- **Location**: Route 28 (post-game, requires all 16 badges)
- **Wild Pokemon**: Ursaring, Donphan, Larvitar, Pupitar, Golduck, Misdreavus, Sneasel, Golbat, Quagsire
- **Legendary Boss**: Red (Trainer Red) at summit — Pikachu Lv81, Espeon Lv73, Snorlax Lv75, Venusaur Lv77, Charizard Lv77, Blastoise Lv77
- **Items**: Calcium, Max Elixir, Escape Rope, Full Restore, Ultra Ball
- **Fly Point**: Yes
