## Johto Dungeons — Internal Connections

### Sprout Tower
- 1F -> 2F (3 staircases at positions (6,4), (2,6), (17,3))
- 2F -> 3F (staircase at (10,14))
- 1F entrance from Violet City (23,5)

### Slowpoke Well
- B1F entrance from Azalea Town (31,7)
- B1F -> B2F (ladder at (7,11); requires Surf)
- B2F -> B1F (ladder at (9,11))

### Union Cave
- 1F south entrance from Route 32 (6,79)
- 1F north exit to Route 33 (17,31)
- 1F -> B1F (two staircases at (5,19) and (3,33))
- B1F -> Ruins of Alph Outside (two warps at (3,3) and (3,11))
- B1F -> B2F (staircase at (17,31))
- B2F -> B1F (staircase at (5,3))
- B2F has Friday Lapras

### Burned Tower
- 1F entrance from Ecruteak City (5,5)
- 1F -> B1F (falling through floor at (10,9))
- Multiple legacy warps from Gold/Silver marked inaccessible in Crystal

### Ilex Forest
- East: Ilex Forest Azalea Gate (from Azalea Town)
- West: Route 34 Ilex Forest Gate (to Route 34)

### Mt. Mortar
- 3 entrances from Route 42: west (10,5), middle (28,9), east (46,7)
- 1F Outside -> 1F Inside (multiple connections)
- 1F Inside -> B1F
- B1F -> 2F Inside (Karate King area)

### Ice Path
- West entrance: Route 44 (56,7) -> Ice Path 1F
- East exit: Ice Path 1F (36,27) -> Blackthorn City
- 1F -> B1F (staircases at (37,5) and (37,13))
- B1F -> B2F Mahogany Side (staircase + fall holes)
- B2F Mahogany Side -> B3F (ladder at (9,11))
- B3F -> B2F Blackthorn Side (ladder at (15,5))
- B2F Blackthorn Side -> B1F (ladder at (3,15))
- B1F -> B2F Blackthorn Side (ladder at (11,27))
- Contains HM07 Waterfall

### Tin Tower (Bell Tower)
- 1F entrance from Ecruteak City (37,7)
- 1F -> 2F -> 3F -> 4F -> 5F -> 6F -> 7F -> 8F -> 9F -> Roof
- Multiple staircases per floor creating a maze
- 7F has internal teleport warps
- Roof: Ho-Oh encounter

### Dragon's Den
- Entrance from Blackthorn City (20,1)
- Dragon's Den 1F has internal warps (ladder)
- 1F -> B1F (water area, requires Surf)
- B1F (19,29) -> Dragon Shrine (quiz location)

### Whirl Islands
- 4 entrances from Route 41 (NW, NE, SW, SE)
- NW -> B1F connecting passage
- NE -> B1F connecting passage
- SW -> dead end with Ultra Ball
- SE -> primary path to Lugia
- Internal: B1F -> B2F -> Lugia's Chamber
- Complex multi-entrance dungeon

### Team Rocket Base
- Mahogany Mart 1F -> B1F (hidden staircase)
- B1F -> B2F -> B3F

### Victory Road
- Route 22 (13,5) / Route 26 (7,5) -> Victory Road Gate
- Victory Road Gate -> Victory Road cave
- Route 28 (33,5) -> Victory Road Gate (Mt. Silver side)
