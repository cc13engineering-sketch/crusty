# Pokemon Crystal — Speedrun Routing Notes

> Source: speedrun.com, Pokemon Speedruns Wiki, community guides
> Purpose: Optimal routing for Any% Glitchless speedruns
> Category: Any% Glitchless (most common competitive category)
> World Record: ~3:15 (Keizaron)

---

## Overview

Pokemon Crystal Any% Glitchless aims to beat the Elite Four and enter the Hall of Fame as fast as possible without using any glitches. The run takes approximately 3:15-4:00 depending on RNG and execution.

### Key Decisions
- **Starter**: Totodile (standard route) — Feraligatr has the best combination of physical stats, Surf STAB, and movepool for sweeping the game
- **Additional Pokemon**: Minimal — catch Kenya the Spearow (Route 35 NPC gift) for Fly, a Surf slave if needed
- **Shopping**: Highly optimized; buy only what's needed
- **Trainer Avoidance**: Skip all optional trainers

## Game Setup

### Options
- Text Speed: **Fast**
- Battle Scene: **Off** (saves animation time)
- Battle Style: **Set** (saves time vs Switch prompt, forces strategic play)
- Time: Set to **5:50 PM** (specific encounter table manipulation)

### Starter Selection
- Choose **Totodile**
- Check stats: Need at minimum 10 Special Attack DV (12+ preferred)
- Attack DV should be reasonable (10+)
- Female Totodile has slightly lower Attack range but works

## Early Game Routing

### New Bark Town → Elm's Lab
1. Go downstairs, get Pokegear from Mom
2. Go to Elm's Lab, select Totodile (right Poke Ball)
3. Check stats on status screen
4. Get Potion from aide
5. Exit east... wait, west to Route 29

### Route 29 → Cherrygrove → Mr. Pokemon
6. Walk to Cherrygrove (avoid tall grass by walking on edges)
7. Get Map Card from Guide Gent (mandatory)
8. Head north to Route 30
9. Skip all optional trainers
10. Mr. Pokemon's House → Mystery Egg + Pokedex

### Rival Battle 1
11. Silver has Chikorita Lv5 (type advantage for us)
12. Spam Scratch/Leer → kill with Scratch
13. Leer first turn is optimal to guarantee 3HKO

### Return to Elm
14. Run back to Elm's Lab
15. Name rival (single character, e.g., "A" — fastest)
16. Get 5 Poke Balls

### Feraligatr's E4 Moveset
The ideal Feraligatr moveset for the E4:
- **Surf** (STAB, 95 power, 100 accuracy)
- **Ice Punch** (75 power, handles Dragon/Grass/Flying)
- **ThunderPunch** (75 power, handles Water/Flying)
- **Strength** or **Cut** (HM, filler for normal-resist situations)

## Time-Saving Tips

### Movement Optimization
- Hold B to run (bicycle replaces this after Goldenrod)
- Take shortest paths — hug walls to avoid grass
- Bicycle is faster than running (get it in Goldenrod)
- Fly to skip backtracking wherever possible

### Battle Optimization
- Never grind — fight only mandatory battles
- Use items during free turns (Hyper Beam recharge, opponent using non-damaging moves)
- Flee from wild encounters immediately
- One-shot everything with super-effective moves

### Shopping Optimization
- Buy in bulk at Goldenrod Dept Store (one trip)
- TM Ice Punch + ThunderPunch are the most important purchases
- Potions/Super Potions only — Full Restores are expensive and rarely needed

### RNG Management
- Critical hits can save or waste time
- Paralysis/Confusion from enemy moves can lose runs
- Whitney's Miltank is the biggest RNG factor in early game
- Avoid accuracy-lowering moves stacking (Mud-Slap, SmokeScreen)

## Common Run-Killers

1. **Whitney's Miltank** — Rollout chains can sweep your team
2. **Rival in Victory Road** — If underleveled, he can end the run
3. **Lance's Dragonite** — If Ice Punch doesn't OHKO, Thunder Wave + Hyper Beam can kill
4. **Clair's Kingdra** — No super-effective coverage; war of attrition
5. **Random critical hits** — Enemy crits on key Pokemon can force resets
6. **Hypnosis/Sleep** — Morty, Chuck, Will's Jynx all use sleep moves

## Category Variants

### Any% (with Glitches)
- Uses Coin Case glitch for arbitrary code execution
- Can beat the game in ~5 minutes
- Not representative of normal gameplay

### Any% Glitchless (Manipless)
- No RNG manipulation
- Longer due to suboptimal DVs and encounter RNG
- Times around 3:30-4:00

### Any% Glitchless (Manipulated)
- Pre-set RNG seed for perfect DVs and encounters
- Catches Raikou early for sweeping
- Fastest legitimate category

### Catch 'Em All
- Must obtain all 251 Pokemon
- 20+ hour runs
- Requires extensive trading and version-exclusive planning
