## Immunities

- Normal and Fighting cannot hit Ghost
- Electric cannot hit Ground
- Poison cannot hit Steel
- Ground cannot hit Flying
- Psychic cannot hit Dark

## Special Mechanics

- **Foresight/Odor Sleuth:** Removes Ghost-type immunities to Normal and Fighting
- **Sandstorm:** Damages all types except Rock, Ground, and Steel for 1/8 max HP per turn
