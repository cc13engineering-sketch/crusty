################################################################################
SECTION 3: STORY PROGRESSION GATES (what blocks what)
################################################################################

Gate 1: Elm's Errand
  Trigger:  Start of game
  Blocker:  Cannot leave New Bark Town until receiving starter from Elm
  Clears:   Obtaining starter Pokemon + Potion

Gate 2: Mr. Pokemon's Egg
  Trigger:  Arrive at Mr. Pokemon's house (Route 30)
  Blocker:  Must deliver Mystery Egg to Elm before progressing
  Clears:   Receive Pokedex from Oak, 5 Poke Balls from Elm's aide

Gate 3: Dark Cave
  Blocker:  Requires Flash (HM05) to navigate; optional area
  Clears:   After Zephyr Badge

Gate 4: Sprout Tower
  Blocker:  None (optional but recommended before gym)
  Clears:   HM05 Flash from Sage Li

Gate 5: Violet Gym
  Blocker:  None
  Clears:   Zephyr Badge -> Flash usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM31 Mud-Slap, Mystery Egg from Elm's aide

Gate 6: Ruins of Alph
  Blocker:  Most puzzles require Surf (later in game)
  Clears:   Unown encounters (optional)

Gate 7: Union Cave
  Blocker:  Lower levels require Surf (later)
  Clears:   Path to Route 33 / Azalea Town

Gate 8: Slowpoke Well
  Trigger:  Arrive in Azalea Town
  Blocker:  Team Rocket controls the well; must defeat them
  Clears:   Kurt gives Lure Ball, access to Azalea Gym

Gate 9: Azalea Gym
  Blocker:  Must clear Slowpoke Well first
  Clears:   Hive Badge -> Cut usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM49 Fury Cutter

Gate 10: Ilex Forest
  Blocker:  Requires Cut (from HM01 after Slowpoke Well)
  Also:     Must catch/guide Farfetch'd back to charcoal maker
  Clears:   Path to Route 34 / Goldenrod City
  Reward:   TM02 Headbutt

Gate 11: Goldenrod Gym
  Blocker:  None
  Clears:   Plain Badge -> Strength usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM45 Attract

Gate 12: Flower Shop / Sudowoodo
  Trigger:  Defeat Whitney
  Blocker:  Sudowoodo blocks Route 36 until sprayed with SquirtBottle
  Clears:   SquirtBottle from Flower Shop (requires Plain Badge)
  Result:   Battle Sudowoodo (Lv.20 Rock-type), path to Route 37 opens

Gate 13: Burned Tower
  Trigger:  Enter Ecruteak City
  Blocker:  Must explore Burned Tower basement to release legendary beasts
  Also:     Rival battle inside (Silver)
  Clears:   Raikou, Entei, Suicune begin roaming; access to Ecruteak Gym

Gate 14: Ecruteak Gym + Dance Theater
  Blocker:  Must defeat Kimono Girls (5 Eevee evolutions) at Dance Theater
  Clears:   Fog Badge -> Surf usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM30 Shadow Ball, HM03 Surf (from old man after Kimono Girls)

Gate 15: Olivine Lighthouse
  Trigger:  Arrive in Olivine City
  Blocker:  Jasmine stays with sick Ampharos; won't battle at gym
  Clears:   Must Surf to Cianwood to get Secret Potion from pharmacy

Gate 16: Cianwood Gym
  Blocker:  None (can challenge Chuck immediately)
  Clears:   Storm Badge -> Fly usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM01 DynamicPunch, HM02 Fly (from Chuck's wife outside gym)

Gate 17: Return to Olivine
  Trigger:  Obtain Secret Potion from Cianwood pharmacy
  Blocker:  Must deliver potion to Jasmine at lighthouse
  Clears:   Jasmine returns to gym; Olivine Gym now open

Gate 18: Olivine Gym
  Blocker:  Must cure Ampharos first
  Clears:   Mineral Badge
  Reward:   TM23 Iron Tail

Gate 19: Lake of Rage
  Trigger:  Arrive in Mahogany Town, head north
  Blocker:  Red Gyarados event; must battle/capture it
  Clears:   Meet Lance, begin Rocket HQ infiltration

Gate 20: Rocket HQ (Mahogany)
  Trigger:  Meet Lance at Lake of Rage
  Blocker:  Must disable signal broadcasting machine (3 Electrode)
  Clears:   Mahogany Gym opens, HM06 Whirlpool from Lance

Gate 21: Mahogany Gym
  Blocker:  Must clear Rocket HQ first
  Clears:   Glacier Badge -> Whirlpool usable outside battle
  Reward:   TM16 Icy Wind

Gate 22: Radio Tower Takeover
  Trigger:  After Mahogany Gym, return to Goldenrod
  Blocker:  Team Rocket controls Radio Tower; must clear all 5 floors
  Also:     Must rescue Director from Underground Warehouse
  Clears:   Rainbow Wing (Gold) or Silver Wing (Silver)
            Route 44 fully accessible

Gate 23: Ice Path
  Blocker:  Requires Strength to push boulders through ice puzzles
  Clears:   Path to Blackthorn City, HM07 Waterfall found inside

Gate 24: Blackthorn Gym
  Blocker:  None
  Clears:   Rising Badge -> Waterfall usable outside battle
  BUT:      Clair refuses to give badge; must complete Dragon's Den test

Gate 25: Dragon's Den
  Trigger:  Defeat Clair but she refuses badge
  Blocker:  Must retrieve Dragon Fang from Dragon's Den (Surf + Whirlpool)
  Clears:   Clair awards Rising Badge + TM24 DragonBreath

Gate 26: Victory Road
  Blocker:  All 8 badges required; Waterfall needed in Tohjo Falls
  Clears:   Path to Indigo Plateau

SECTION 4: DETAILED CITY DATA — NEW BARK TOWN
################################################################################

Slogan: "The Town Where Winds of a New Beginning Blow"

Connections:
  West -> Route 29
  East -> Route 27 (requires Surf; late-game)

Buildings:
  1. Player's House
     - Bedroom: PC, bed, radio, N64 (decoration)
     - Ground floor: Mom saves money, buys decorations
  2. Professor Elm's Lab
     - Bookshelves, computer, Pokemon restoration machine
     - Table with 3 Poke Balls (starters)
  3. Rival's House (Silver's house in Gen II)
     - His mother/grandmother lives here

Key NPCs:
  Mom (Player's Mother)
    - Gives Pokegear at game start
    - Saves portion of prize money when called
    - Buys decorations for bedroom (dolls, beds, carpets)
  Professor Elm
    - Gives starter Pokemon (Chikorita/Cyndaquil/Totodile, Lv.5)
    - Gives 1 Potion (via aide)
    - Gives Everstone (after showing hatched Togepi)
    - Gives Master Ball (after all 8 badges)
    - Gives S.S. Ticket (after Hall of Fame)
  Elm's Aide
    - Gives 1 Potion when leaving lab first time
    - Gives 5 Poke Balls after first rival battle

Items:
  - Pokegear (from Mom)
  - Potion x1 (from Elm's aide)
  - Berry (held by starter Pokemon)
  - Poke Ball x5 (from aide, after defeating Silver)
  - Everstone (from Elm, after Togepi hatches)
  - Master Ball (from Elm, after 8 badges)
  - S.S. Ticket (from Elm, post-game)

Story Events (chronological):
  1. Game begins in player's bedroom
  2. Go downstairs, receive Pokegear from Mom
  3. Visit Elm's lab, select starter Pokemon (Lv.5)
  4. See Silver spying through lab window
  5. Elm's aide gives Potion
  6. Elm asks player to visit Mr. Pokemon on Route 30
  7. Depart west via Route 29
  --- LATER ---
  8. Return after Mr. Pokemon errand, find lab was robbed
  9. Name the rival (Silver), give Elm the Mystery Egg
  10. Receive Pokedex, 5 Poke Balls
  --- MUCH LATER ---
  11. Return with Togepi -> Everstone
  12. Return with 8 badges -> Master Ball
  13. After Elite Four -> S.S. Ticket

Pokemon Encounters (water, available late-game with Surf):
  Surfing:    Tentacool (Lv.15-24, 90%), Tentacruel (Lv.20-24, 10%)
  Old Rod:    Magikarp (Lv.10, 85%), Tentacool (Lv.10, 15%)
  Good Rod:   Magikarp (Lv.20, 35%), Tentacool (Lv.20, 35%),
              Shellder (Lv.20, 10%), Chinchou (Lv.20, 20%)
  Super Rod:  Chinchou (Lv.40, 40%), Shellder (Lv.40, 30%),
              Tentacruel (Lv.40, 20%), Lanturn (Lv.40, 10%)

Slogan: "The Festive City of Opulent Charm"
Description: "A developing city where people and Pokemon come and go as they like."
Note: Largest city in Johto. Major economic center.

Connections:
  North -> Route 35
  South -> Route 34

Buildings:
  1. Pokemon Center
     - Standard healing

     - 5F: TM Corner (elemental punches, $3,000)
     - 6F: Rooftop Square (vending machines)
     - B1F: Connected to Goldenrod Tunnel
     - Sunday: TM27 Return or TM21 Frustration (friendship-based)
  3. Goldenrod Gym
     - Leader: Whitney (Normal-type)
     - Flower maze layout
  4. Radio Tower (5 floors)
     - 1F: Lucky Number Lottery, Radio Card quiz
     - 2F+: Story-locked until Radio Tower takeover
     - Broadcasts: Pokemon Talk, Pokemon Music, Lucky Channel
  5. Game Corner
     - Slot machines (1-3 coins/play)
     - Card Flip game (Gen II exclusive)
     - Coin purchase: 50 for $1,000 or 500 for $10,000
     - Prizes:
         Abra Lv.10         — 200 coins
         Ekans Lv.10 (Gold) / Sandshrew Lv.10 (Silver) — 700 coins
         Dratini Lv.10      — 2,100 coins
         TM25 Thunder       — 5,500 coins
         TM14 Blizzard      — 5,500 coins
         TM38 Fire Blast    — 5,500 coins
  6. Magnet Train Station
     - Connects Goldenrod to Saffron City (Kanto)
     - Requires Pass (post-game)
  7. Goldenrod Tunnel (Underground)
     - Connects SW to NW city
     - Shops:
       Herbal Shop (weekends only):
         Energy Root $800, Energy Powder $500
         Revival Herb $2,800, Heal Powder $450
       Pokemon Salon (Haircut Brothers):
