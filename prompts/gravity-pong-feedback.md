Notes regarding a list of things that must be different in the next version of gravity pong:

- Currently waypoints must only work on the closest particle.
- Releasing the sling does NOT shoot the ball. The whole point is to shoot.
- No two waypoints can exist at the same time.
- when pulling back the sling, the player should be able to do the pulling drag gesture from anywjere - think "like minigolf"
- The game is very "pixel"-y if that makes sense. Is there a way to maybe render more densely and get a smoother cleaner visual finish?
- Use opaque color gradients on contour graph visuals when multiple obstacles (repulsor, gravity well, etc) modify the gravitational fields of a level.
- We must offer a mechanic by which the player can "see" the radius of their waypoints before setting them. THey do this by dragging before releasing - during drag they get an outlined radius of their way point.
